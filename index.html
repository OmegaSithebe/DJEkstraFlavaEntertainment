About Ekstra Flava

DJ Ekstra Flava offers top-tier entertainment, technical production, and event management services for corporate, private, and public events. With over 20 years of experience, we bring the best in sound, visuals, and performances to make your event unforgettable.
Starting out as a DJ at around 14 or 15 years old, I began by playing at birthday parties and school events. This led to my involvement in promotions and performances for major brands like Surf, Colgate, Coca-Cola, and SAB on roadshows. I also built a local following by performing in neighborhood taverns and pubs. After high school, I fully immersed myself in the entertainment industry, working as a sound and audiovisual technician. I advanced to Sales and Event Management at Eventpro, one of South Africa's largest production companies, and freelanced for other top production companies such as MEG Productions, On-Cue Productions, and Sound Level Media. Through this journey, I have crafted unforgettable experiences for events across the country.
